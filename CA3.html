A Human's Guide to CyberSecurity

Find out before they get in

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the protection of internet-connected systems (like computers, phones, and networks) from digital attacks.
Data Protection
It keeps data, devices, and networks safe from hackers, viruses, and other threats.
Preventing Threats
Helps prevent unauthorized access, theft, and damage to your personal or business information.
Personal Information Security
Includes protecting things like emails, passwords, online accounts, and bank details.
Why It Matters
Important for everyone—not just tech experts—because we all use the internet daily.
Good Cyber Habits
Cybersecurity involves safe habits, updated software, and strong passwords.

Why do we need CyberSecurity?

Think of the internet like a big city—fun, fast, and full of surprises. But just like in a real city, there are also pickpockets, scammers, and tricksters. That’s why we need cybersecurity. It’s like having locks, alarms, and bodyguards for your digital life. Learn how to protect yourself before something goes wrong.
