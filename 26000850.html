Safe practices
Things you can do to stay safe on social media.

Social media is fun, but it's important to stay safe while using it.

Top Safety Tips

No. Tip Why It’s Important
1 Use strong and unique passwords for each account If you reuse the same password, a hacker who gets one can access all your accounts. Strong passwords with letters, numbers, and symbols are harder to guess.
2 Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) Even if someone knows your password, they can’t log in without the second step (like a code sent to your phone), which gives extra protection.
3 Keep your personal information private Sharing things like your birthday, school, or address makes it easier for scammers or stalkers to find or impersonate you.
4 Don’t accept friend requests or DMs from strangers They might trick you, send fake links, or try to gain your trust to scam or harm you. Only connect with people you really know.
5 Be cautious of phishing scams or suspicious links Phishing links often look real but steal your passwords or install malware. Always double-check links before clicking.
6 Review and adjust your privacy settings often Apps sometimes change settings without notice. Reviewing helps you control who sees your posts and keeps your data safer.
7 Log out from accounts when using shared devices If you stay logged in, the next user can access your private messages or even post as you. Always log out to protect yourself.

Quick Checklist

  1. Think before posting
  2. Check your privacy settings
  3. Use strong passwords

Key Points Summary

  • Protect personal info
  • Don’t click unknown links
  • Adjust settings regularly
